Legal & Compliance

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 10, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to MealFlow. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on our platform. This includes:

  • Personal Credentials: Username, password hash, and full name.
  • Contact Metadata: Email addresses and phone numbers.
  • Delivery Information: Full physical addresses and 6-digit delivery pincodes.
  • Preferences: Diet categories (Veg, Non-Veg).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information collected via our platform for a variety of business purposes, including:

  • To facilitate account creation, authentication, and login processes.
  • To deliver hot meals to your specified address.
  • To process checkout sessions and subscriptions dynamically through our billing gateways.
  • To evaluate customer inquiries, ratings, and issue reports.

4. Stripe Integration & Payment Safety

We utilize Stripe as our third-party checkout provider. When you subscribe to a meal plan, your payment information is entered directly into Stripe's secure portal. MealFlow does not collect, record, or store any card numbers, cardholder verification values (CVV), or bank details.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We will only keep your personal information for as long as it is necessary for the purposes set out in this privacy policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. Users can contact our customer support team to request deletion of their account records.

For privacy inquiries, reach out to hello@mealflow.in
